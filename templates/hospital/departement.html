Our Departements

Cardologist

The cardiology department is dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart-related diseases, with the goal of improving the cardiovascular health and overall well-being of patients.

Anesthesiologist

Anesthesiologist is a specialized medical doctor who plays a vital role in the perioperative care of patients. Their expertise and knowledge contribute to the successful and smooth execution of surgical procedures.

Dermatologist

Dermatologists are medical experts who specialize in diagnosing, treating, and managing conditions that affect the skin, hair, nails, and mucous membranes.

Allergists

The Allergists Department is a specialized medical unit within a healthcare facility dedicated to the diagnosis, management, and treatment of allergies and related conditions. Allergists, also known as immunologists.

Orthopedists

The Orthopedics Department, also known as Orthopedic Surgery Department, is a specialized medical unit within a healthcare facility that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and musculoskeletal disorders and injuries.

Emergency Medicine Specialists

Emergency medicine specialists are medical doctors who specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of acute and urgent medical conditions.

Oncologists

Oncologists are medical doctors who specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of cancer. They are trained to investigate, diagnose, and manage various types of cancer, as well as provide ongoing care for cancer patients.

Pediatricians

Pediatricians are doctors who specialize in the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents. They are trained to diagnose, treat, and prevent a wide range of childhood illnesses, injuries, and developmental issues.

Psychiatrists

Psychiatrists are medical doctors who specialize in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental, emotional, and behavioral disorders.

Nephrologists

Nephrologists are medical doctors who specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of kidney-related conditions and diseases. They are trained to evaluate and manage various disorders that affect the kidneys.
